Anxiety Attack Help


Anxiety- something that all humans experience and that is a normal part of life. For symptoms, look at the stress symptoms page.
Panic Attack- sudden feelings of terror which can strike without warning. People experiencing an attack may believe they are having a heart attack or going crazy. Symptoms may include: racing heart, fast breathing, feeling weak, tingling or numbness, sense of terror
If you have an anxiety attack- Goal is to calm down, slow down, and slow breathing. Tips:
  1. Lollipop (helps you close mouth and slow breathing)
  2. Keep hands busy with items like stress balls (doing something with hands will switch focus from cause of anxiety)
  3. Try to list something from each of 5 senses (move focus from stress)
  4. Try to have a distracting conversation with someone
Get to office as quickly as possible for best chance of recovery!
