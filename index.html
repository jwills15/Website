Welcome to my Website!

Welcome to my website! My name is Joshua Williams and I am an electrical engineering student at Biola University, which is a part of USC's 3+2 program. After three years, I will transfer to USC to finish my electrical engineering degree. I made this website in order to showcase the projects that I made as a part of the Global Information Technology Academy (GITA), a computer science program at my high school. I was in GITA for three years, and I learned C#, HTML/CSS, Javascript, and Java. I also took the AP Computer Science test and passed it with a 5. This website was designed to be viewed on desktop, but I have included Bootstrap in order to make my website presentable on mobile devices. All my projects from my three years of GITA are included on this website. To read more about me, click on the "About Me" tab on the navigation bar above. Enjoy!
