My Personal Portfolio: CS and Research

My name is Joshua Williams and I am a Computer Engineering and Computer Science student at the University of Southern California. I transferred to USC as a part of the 3+2 program. I previously studied Engineering Physics at Biola University. I initially made this website in high school to showcase the projects that I made as a part of the Global Information Technology Academy (GITA), a computer science program at my high school. I was in GITA for three years, and I learned C#, HTML/CSS, Javascript, and Java. I also took the AP Computer Science test and passed it with a 5. This website was designed to be viewed on a desktop, but I have included Bootstrap to make my website presentable on mobile devices. All my projects from my three years of GITA are included on this website. I have also included links to new projects that I have been working on in college. To read more about me, click on the "About Me" tab on the navigation bar above.
