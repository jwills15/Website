Stress Relief Tips

Tips for Decreasing Stress/Anxiety in Your Life

Write down your schedule and assess a typical day. If there is no downtime to relax, there needs to be a change.
  1. Get enough sleep (6-8 hours per night)
  2. Eat healthy food that nourishes your body
  3. Exercise
  4. Turn off and relax for at least 20 minutes a day
  5. Learn a repetitive activity like knitting or crochet
  6. Journal
  7. Write down a "to do" list that is running in your brain to get it out
  8. Reframe a problem and prepare for possible solutions but leave it for later
  9. Get professional help if necessary
