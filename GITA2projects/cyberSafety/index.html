Welcome to the Ninja Cyber Safety Academy

Welcome to the Ninja Cyber Safety Academy, produced by the Global IT Academy (GITA) of Brea Olinda High School. We try to educate people about the dangers of the internet and how to stay safe. Levels 2 and 3 of the GITA program each produce a website to add to the Ninja Cyber Safety Academy.

Our group is focused on using social media. There are three lessons, each with a video and a lesson, as well as a quiz at the end to see how much you learned. Watch the intro video or click on a lesson to start. Thank you for visiting!

Lessons

Lesson 1: Using Social Media Effectively

Social media is used by people of all ages for various purposes. We will teach you how to use it most effectively.

Lesson 1

Lesson 2: Social Media and Health

Social media is a lot of fun to use and promotes human interaction, but it can also cause stress. We will guide you through maximizing the enjoyment of social media.

Lesson 2

Lesson 3: Social Media and Jobs

Everything posted on social media can be seen by an employer, so we will teach you how to be safe with what you do on social media.
