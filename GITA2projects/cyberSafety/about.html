About Ninja Cyber Safety Academy

Ninja Cyber Safety Academy is a website created and run by the Global IT Academy (GITA) at BOHS. GITA is a selective 4-year program which teaches four different languages of computer science, culminating in taking the AP Computer Science test.

The Ninja Cyber Safety Academy was created in order to help people of all ages learn how to be safe on the internet. Each year levels 2 and 3 of the GITA program are assigned to make their own addition to the Ninja Cyber Safety Academy. GITA 2 creates websites with HTML5, and GITA 3 creates websites with Flash. Click here to visit the other Ninja Cyber Safety Academy websites.

Our group is from GITA 2. Our website for the Ninja Cyber Safety Academy is focused on teaching how to use social media. We hope that you are able to learn a lot from using our website.
